CHICAGO  Grain futures were mixed Tuesday on the Chicago Board of Trade.

Wheat for March delivery rose 4.75 cents to $6.0525 a bushel; March corn was 1.50 cents lower at 4.22 a bushel; March oats rose .50 cent to $3.5425 a bushel; while March soybeans declined 16.25 cents to $12.9250 a bushel.

Beef lower and pork higher on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange.

February live cattle fell .48 cent to $1.3462 a pound; March feeder cattle was .55 cent lower at $1.6740 a pound; while February lean hogs rose .45 cent to $.8542 a pound.
